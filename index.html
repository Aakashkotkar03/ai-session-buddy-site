What you get

Everything a power user needs

Six tools built into one extension. Each one does something specific so you stay in flow.

🗂
Session Manager
Save any mix of windows and tabs as a named session. Restore everything exactly as it was — in one click. Color-code and annotate for context.
📊
Analytics & Reports
See how your time splits across Development, Learning, Social, and 12 other categories. Charts, scores, and patterns — updated automatically.
⏱
Time Machine
Auto-snapshots every 30 minutes. Go back to any point, compare two moments side-by-side, and restore any past state of your browser.
📌
Research Board
Save any page while browsing with one click. Add notes, highlight text, tag and star items. Your private, offline research hub.
🧠
Knowledge Graph
Watch your browsing history turn into a living visual map of topics and connections. See which subjects you visit together and how focus shifts over time.
🔔
Daily Digest
An 8 PM summary of what you did today — time spent, top category, productivity score, and any milestones. Private, in your notification bell.
